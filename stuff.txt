GAME LOGIC

PARTY SETUP
The number of parties is between 2 and 8 (inclusive).
The total number of seats at the start is 120 (119 if 7 players).
Each party starts with an equal proportion of seats.
Each party also starts with $2000 and 100 Support.

PARTY STATS:
Each party has these stats:
Seats:
- Used when voting on bills.
- Seats are gained or lost during elections.
Money:
- Used to complete actions.
- Money can be gained and lost through actions.
Support: 
- Used to determine election results. 
- Support can be gained and lost through actions.

ACTIONS:
# RALLY: Exchange money for support.
- Small rally: -$100 => +10 + rand(-2~10) Support
- Medium rally: -$500 => +50 + rand(-10~50) Support
- Large rally: -$1000 => +100 + rand(-20~100) Support
# FUNDRAISER: Earn money based on support.
- +1000 * (own Support/sum(Support)) + rand(-500~500)
# BILL: Propose a bill to be voted on later.
- Change electoral term: change how often elections are. (1 year ~ 10 years)
- Snap election: hold an election right away. 

ELECTIONS:
- Elections take place every 4 years by default; this can be changed via a bill.
- Each party gets a proportion of the total number of seats.
- The proportion is equal to their support over the sum of all supports.
- After each election, the turn order changes: most seats go first.
